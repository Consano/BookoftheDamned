# Spirit Split `[two-actions]`      Spell 5
==Uncommon== ==Necromancy== ==Void== ==Healing==
**Traditions** occult, divine
**Range** 30ft cone
**Defense** Fortitude **Duration** 2 Rounds

---
You split the spirits of nearby creatures from their bodies rendering them vulnerable to outside influence.

---
All non-spirit creatures within range must attempt a Fortitude save or have their spirits split from their bodies. Until the affect ends the creatures spirit occupies their space and can be affected by spells. Any change in the spirits HP is also reflected in the creature they originate from as Spirit Healing or Spirit Damage

Spirits have the following extra benefits but are otherwise identical to the creature they originate from:
**Immunities** bleed, death effects, disease, paralyzed, poison, precision, unconscious; 
**Resistances** all damage 5 (except force, ghost touch, spirit, or vitality; double resistance vs. non-magical)
